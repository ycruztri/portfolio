About Me

Hello! I am Yulianna Cruz-Trinidad, and I am from San Diego, California. I am currently a student at Brown University studying computer science, interested in a full stack software engineer career.

My journey into the field of software engineering started during a transformative middle school field trip, where I had the privilege of engaging with a software engineer. Her profound dedication and articulate exposition of her projects left a lasting impression, inspiring me to pursue a similar path with unwavering determination and enthusiasm.

In my free time, I love going to the beach, playing card games, or taking pictures of beautiful scenery.
