Responsive Redesign

Redesigning a webpage for a local restaurant

Role

  • Designer
  • Developer

Tools

  • Figma
  • VSCode

Context/Problem

Bagel Gourmet Cafe, known for its delicious breakfast sandwiches, lacked an online presence that matched the quality of its offerings. The existing website suffered from usability issues, including poor accessibility, responsiveness, and visual design, hindering user engagement and potentially driving away customers.

Research

Conducting an analysis of the website revealed key points:

  • User frustration due to difficulty in accessing essential information like menu, location, and contact details.
  • Challenges navigating the website on smaller screens.
  • Visual clutter and lack of hierarchy, making it hard for users to focus on important content.
  • Accessibility issues, including poor color contrast and missing alt text for images, affecting users with disabilities.
